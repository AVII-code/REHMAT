A special beginning ✨

Maybe you'll remember this better than I do... the day we first met at the tuition centre. At that time, we were just two strangers sitting in the same place, living completely different lives. If someone had told me that one day I'd be writing an entire book for you, I would've laughed and said, "Impossible." But life has its own plans. We didn't become close instantly. It started with simple conversations, random jokes, and slowly getting to know each other. Without even realizing it, those little moments became the beginning of something I never wanted to lose. The funny part is... I thought I was just making a new friend. I had no idea I was meeting someone who would one day become my comfort, my peace, and one of the happiest parts of my life. Looking back now, I don't think our story started because of luck. I think some people are simply meant to cross paths, no matter how ordinary the beginning looks. And if I ever got the chance to relive that day... I'd still walk into that tuition centre... Just to meet you all over again. ❤️

From Strangers to Something Special ❤️✨

It's still crazy to me how two complete strangers ended up becoming this close. There wasn't a grand moment where everything changed. It was just one conversation after another... one laugh after another... one memory after another, Without even realizing it, "Kyu tuhi didi ro sun ni" slowly turned into hours of talking, and "Take care" started carrying more meaning than either of us probably noticed. I still smile when I think about those early days. We were just getting to know each other, yet it somehow felt so easy to talk to you. You never felt like someone I had to impress. You simply became someone I wanted to know a little more every single day. The best part is that we never forced anything. Our friendship happened naturally, and maybe that's why it's so beautiful. We weren't looking for something special... but somehow, we found it in each other. Sometimes I wonder what would've happened if we had never met. Honestly, I don't even want to imagine that version of my life. Because if meeting you taught me one thing, it's that the most beautiful people often enter your life when you're not even looking for them. And I'm really glad... one of those people was you yk aka june theory ❤️

The Magic of You ✨💖

Do you know what's magical about you? It's not just your smile, your eyes, or how pretty you are .... although I'd be lying if I said those weren't beautiful. The real magic is something you probably don't even notice. It's the way you make people feel. You make ordinary conversations feel unforgettable. You make bad days feel a little lighter without even trying. Your presence has this strange way of calming my mind, and somehow, whenever you're around, life feels less complicated. You care more than you admit. You overthink more than people realize. You pretend to be strong when you're hurting, yet somehow you still find the strength to care about everyone else. That's rare. People can find beauty almost anywhere. But finding someone whose heart is as beautiful as their face... That doesn't happen every day. Maybe that's why you became so special to me without me even realizing it. You're not magic because you're perfect. You're magic because you're you. And honestly... I wouldn't change a single thing about that. ❤️

US 💫✨

It's funny how the word "us" looks so small, yet it carries an entire world inside it. When people ask me about my happiest memories, they probably expect some huge adventure or a perfect day. But honestly... most of my favorite memories begin with one simple thing ... you. Us laughing over something that wasn't even funny. Us teasing each other for absolutely no reason. Us staying silent but somehow understanding each other anyway. Us turning ordinary days into memories without even trying. Some people spend years searching for someone they can truly be themselves around. I got lucky Because with you, I never felt like I had to pretend to be someone else. I could be my weird, annoying, overthinking self... and somehow you stayed. That's what "us" means to me. Not perfection. Not fairy tales. Just two imperfect people who somehow became each other's comfort. And honestly... I wouldn't trade our "us" for anything in this world. ❤️ "The best stories are never about one person... they're always about us."

The Best Part of My Day🤞💖

You know what's funny? Sometimes nothing exciting happens during my day. Classes are boring. Work gets tiring. Everything feels the same. Then your notification pops up... And somehow the whole day feels different. I don't think you realize how many times you've made me smile without even being there. Sometimes it's your random messages. Sometimes it's your voice. Sometimes it's you arguing with me over the smallest things. Sometimes it's just knowing you're okay. You became that one person I unknowingly started looking forward to every day. Not because I needed someone to complete my life... But because you made an already ordinary life feel happier. And that's a beautiful feeling. Thank you for becoming the reason behind so many unexpected smiles. Thank you for making "today" something I look forward to. Because no matter how the day begins... It always becomes a little better when it includes you. "The best part of my day has never been the day itself... it has always been rehana." ❤️

A Wish from the Heart 💫💖

If I were given just one wish for you, I wouldn't ask for a perfect life. I'd wish for peaceful mornings. For genuine smiles. For people who never make you question your worth. For dreams that actually come true. For a heart that never has to beg for the love it deserves. You've spent so much of your life being strong for everyone else that I sometimes wonder who stays strong for you. I hope, from now on, life becomes a little kinder to you. I hope every tear you cried in silence is replaced with a reason to laugh. I hope you never again meet someone who makes you feel like you're "not enough," because the truth is... you've always been more than enough. And selfishly... I hope, years from now, when you think about the people who genuinely wanted the best for you... You remember me. Because seeing you happy has quietly become one of my favorite wishes. "Some prayers are never spoken... they're simply felt." ❤️

Always Here for You 💖✨

I don't know what tomorrow looks like. Life changes. People change. Sometimes even the strongest plans don't go the way we expect. But there's one thing I hope never changes. Whenever life feels too heavy... Whenever you're tired... Whenever you feel like nobody understands you... I hope you remember that you have someone who'll always listen. You don't have to pretend to be okay around me. You don't have to smile just because everyone expects you to. If you're happy, I'll celebrate with you. If you're sad, I'll sit beside you until you're okay again. If you ever lose faith in yourself... I'll remind you of the person I see every single day. A kind heart. A beautiful soul. Someone worth choosing. Always. I can't promise to solve every problem. But I can promise that you should never have to face them alone if I'm still around. Because some people don't just stay during the easy days... They stay when staying actually matters. And I want to be one of those people. "No matter where life takes us... you'll never have to look for me. I'll already be there." ❤️

The Beauty of You ✨💖

People usually think beauty is something they can see. I don't. Yes, you're beautiful. Your smile, your eyes, your little expressions—they're all enough to make anyone stop for a second. But if I'm being honest, those were never my favorite things about you. My favorite version of your beauty is invisible. It's the way you worry about people you love. The way you forgive even when you shouldn't. The way you laugh so freely that everyone around you starts smiling too. The way you care without expecting anything in return. That's the kind of beauty that doesn't fade with time. I've met beautiful faces before. But meeting a beautiful heart... That's rare. And somehow, life decided I deserved to meet yours. If I had to describe you in one sentence, I'd never call you the prettiest girl. I'd say... "She's the kind of person who makes the world feel softer just by being in it." "Your face caught my attention... but your heart quietly became my favorite place." ❤️

You know something? When people ask about goals, they usually talk about careers, money, success, or big achievements. Those things matter. But when I think about the future, I picture something much simpler. I hope we both become people our younger selves would be proud of. I hope we chase our dreams without losing ourselves. I hope we keep laughing at stupid jokes even when life gets serious. I hope no matter how busy life becomes, we never become strangers again. I want to see you achieve everything you've ever dreamed about. I want to be the loudest person clapping when you win. And if life ever becomes difficult... I hope we remind each other why we started. Because success feels good. But having someone you're genuinely happy to share it with... That's priceless. No matter where life takes us, I hope one thing always remains the same— The comfort of knowing that somewhere in this world... We're still rooting for each other. "Some goals aren't about reaching a destination... they're about walking the journey together." ❤️

thoughts abt You🌹❤
